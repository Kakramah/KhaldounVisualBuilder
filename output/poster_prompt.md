## Concept 1: The Silent Archive

- Concept name: The Silent Archive
- Visual metaphor: Memory as a preserved witness, held quietly rather than displayed as spectacle.
- Composition: Vertical poster frame with a dark archival table in the lower third, unmarked papers and a closed folder arranged with restraint, and a large empty shadow field above. The image should feel distinct from the website hero by using a tighter still-life poster composition rather than a wide interior space.
- Lighting: Low-key side light from one warm source, soft falloff, deep blacks, controlled contrast.
- Mood: Serious, humane, patient, documentary, dignified.
- Texture: Matte paper grain, worn cardboard, dust in the light, subtle film grain, realistic surface detail.
- Negative space for Arabic title placement: Broad clean negative space in the upper half, dark but not flat.
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 vertical poster.
- Negative constraints: no visible violence, no people, no readable writing, no names, no dates, no flags, no logos, no text inside the image, no weapons, no blood, no invented location, no specific event scene.

## Concept 2: The Threshold Of Responsibility

- Concept name: The Threshold Of Responsibility
- Visual metaphor: Memory as a passage from silence toward responsibility and prevention.
- Composition: Vertical poster frame showing a dark, non-identifiable threshold or doorway-like shape with a restrained warm light beyond it; the foreground remains empty and still. The composition must avoid looking like `images/prevention.jpg` by being more graphic, vertical, and poster-like, with the threshold centered low and the upper frame held as negative space.
- Lighting: Dim ambient darkness with a narrow warm glow from beyond the threshold, gentle gradients, no dramatic spectacle.
- Mood: Sober, humane, forward-looking, protective, quiet.
- Texture: Rough wall surface, muted floor texture, slight atmospheric haze, fine film grain.
- Negative space for Arabic title placement: Clean upper-right negative space with enough darkness for possible Arabic title overlay later.
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 vertical poster.
- Negative constraints: no visible violence, no people, no identifiable place, no dates, no flags, no logos, no text inside the image, no weapons, no blood, no specific event scene, no landmark.

## Concept 3: The Line Of Light

- Concept name: The Line Of Light
- Visual metaphor: Justice and responsibility as a thin line of clarity crossing the darkness of forgetting.
- Composition: Minimal vertical poster frame with an abstract realistic dark wall or surface; one narrow diagonal line of warm light crosses the lower half and touches a small unmarked archival paper edge. This must differ from `images/justice-shadow.jpg` by being simpler, more iconic, and designed as the strongest standalone poster image rather than a section illustration.
- Lighting: Low-key cinematic lighting, deep shadow, one precise warm light line, controlled contrast, soft edges.
- Mood: Solemn, ethical, restrained, humane, memorable.
- Texture: Aged plaster or dark paper surface, faint scratches, dust particles, subtle film grain, realistic documentary texture.
- Negative space for Arabic title placement: Large clean dark negative space across the upper half and upper-left area.
- Aspect ratio: 3:4 vertical poster.
- Negative constraints: no visible violence, no people, no courtroom symbols, no scales, no readable writing, no dates, no flags, no logos, no text inside the image, no weapons, no blood, no invented location, no specific event scene.

## Comparison

- The Silent Archive is the most directly connected to memory preservation, but it risks feeling close to the archive image slot if the generated image becomes too literal.
- The Threshold Of Responsibility is strong and emotional without violence, but it overlaps conceptually with the prevention image because both rely on a distant light and passage-like symbolism.
- The Line Of Light is the most poster-like and visually distinct: it is symbolic, minimal, cinematic, and strong enough to serve as the project’s main visual asset without duplicating the hero, archive, justice, or prevention images.

## Chosen Best Concept

The chosen concept is: The Line Of Light.

It is the strongest option because it turns the project’s core idea into a simple, memorable visual metaphor: memory, justice, and responsibility held together by one restrained line of clarity. It avoids invented facts, visible violence, people, flags, logos, and specific events. It also leaves generous negative space for Arabic title placement while staying visually different from the wider hero image and the other website image slots.
